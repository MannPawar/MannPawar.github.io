Generative NPC Dialogue from an Opinion-Dynamics ABM

Each line below is generated by a small language model conditioned on an NPC’s emergent political opinion from the agent-based simulation.

How this works. A stochastic agent-based model evolves a political opinion p for each of 1,010 named Skyrim NPCs over a Civil War campaign (p → 0 Imperial, p → 1 Stormcloak). That opinion, not a hand-written script, is mapped to a stance and passed to a local small language model (deepseek-r1:1.5b via Ollama), which voices the character in-context. The ABM is the source of truth; the SLM is a generative front-end.
